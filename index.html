About Our School

Welcome to the School Management System. Our mission is to provide an excellent environment for students and staff, enabling a smooth academic experience and effective school management.School Functions: Annual Day: Celebrated yearly, featuring student performances, awards, and guest speeches to honor achievements. Sports Day: A day dedicated to athletics, games, and physical activities, promoting sportsmanship and teamwork among students. Cultural Festivals: Celebrations of various cultural festivals, promoting inclusivity and awareness of different traditions. Parent-Teacher Meetings: Regular meetings between parents and teachers to discuss student progress, challenges, and goals. Workshops & Seminars: Special sessions aimed at enhancing students’ knowledge on specific topics like science, environment, and career guidance. School Timings: Regular Days: Monday to Friday, from 8:30 AM to 3:00 PM. Saturday (Half Day): From 8:30 AM to 12:30 PM, often dedicated to extra-curricular activities. Breaks: Morning Break: 10:30 AM to 10:45 AM Lunch Break: 12:30 PM to 1:00 PM Special Events: Timings may vary based on the event duration, typically announced in advance.
